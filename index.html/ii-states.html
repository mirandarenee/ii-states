Why this matters

The AI Paradox

AI gets more powerful every month. Cheaper every quarter. But access you can trust? That's getting narrower. A handful of companies, in a handful of countries, control it all.

Centralised

A handful of cloud providers run it all. One outage, one policy change — entire industries go dark.

Opaque

Closed code. Hidden data. No way to check for bias or manipulation. Just trust them.

Fragile

One outage, one ban, one injunction — the AI your hospital depends on disappears.

Intelligence is getting cheaper. Trust is not. That's not a market problem — it's a design failure.

A third path

What Is II-States?

Two paths for AI exist today: corporations own it, or governments control it. II-States is the third path. Every nation builds and runs its own Universal AI, owned by the people it serves.

Think franchise, not startup. Intelligent Internet provides the open-source stack — complete, proven, auditable. Each nation takes it and creates a locally owned AI company — a National Champion — that guarantees its citizens daily access to real AI.

Intelligent Internet never owns these companies. The UAI Company of California. The UAI Company of Kenya. Your AI. Your rules. Your data stays home.

Not corporate AI. Not government AI. A third path — AI by the people, for the people.

What we believe

The Five Principles

Not aspirations — rules. Every piece of the II-Stack traces back to at least one.

Openness

Every line of code, every model weight, every dataset — open-source. If you can't inspect it, you can't trust it.

Credible Neutrality

No hidden levers. Rules are public, objective, the same for everyone. Changes require a transparent vote. No surprises.

Human + Agent Dignity

One account. One AI agent. No identity checks for basic access. Your key, your data, your autonomy.

Sovereign Interoperability

Every II-State is independent. Own rules, own data, own governance. But one shared protocol — so they cooperate without giving up sovereignty.
